Popular places to visit

Paris — The City of Love and Art. Be sure to visit the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre.

Tokyo — A blend of tradition and technology. Stroll through the Shibuya and Asakusa districts.

Rome — The City of History and Ancient Architecture. Don’t miss the Colosseum and the Vatican.

Travel Tips

  • Plan Ahead. Research destinations, book accommodation, and check visa requirements.
  • Travel Light. The less luggage you have, the easier it is to move around and less stress.
  • Store Documents in the Cloud. Make copies of your passport and tickets in Google Drive or iCloud.
  • Learn Basic Phrases in the Local Language. It will help you communicate and show respect for the local culture.
  • Don’t Forget Travel Insurance. It might save your trip in case of emergency.
  • Use Offline Maps. Download maps on Google Maps or Maps.me before you leave.
  • Leave Room for Spontaneity. Sometimes the best moments are the ones that weren’t planned
